What to share when scheduling

Pickup and destination

Full addresses help dispatch confirm route, timing, and driver availability.

Appointment details

Include appointment time, return ride needs, expected wait time, and who should be contacted.

Mobility notes

Share any mobility assistance, walker, wheelchair, escort, or vehicle needs before the ride is confirmed.

Accessibility and special vehicle availability must be confirmed before booking.
