Who We Help

Built for riders, families, and coordinators.

Medical rides often involve more than a pickup and drop-off. SafeCare collects the details needed to confirm timing, contact information, destination instructions, mobility notes, and whether a return trip is needed.

  • Private-pay riders going to medical appointments
  • Families scheduling rides for parents or loved ones
  • Senior communities and care coordinators
  • Facilities that need repeatable ride intake details

Important service note

SafeCare is for non-emergency transportation. If there is a medical emergency, call 911. Wheelchair service, ADA-accessible vehicle availability, broker enrollment, and specialized medical transport requirements must be confirmed before booking.

FAQ

Medical ride questions.

Is this ambulance service?

No. SafeCare is for non-emergency medical transportation. For emergencies, call 911.

Can a family member schedule for a rider?

Yes. Families and caregivers can request rides and provide appointment, pickup, return ride, and contact details.

Can facilities request recurring rides?

Yes. Clinics, rehab centers, senior communities, and care coordinators can discuss recurring transportation needs and documentation expectations.

Do you provide wheelchair transportation?

Wheelchair service and ADA-accessible vehicle availability must be confirmed before booking and should not be assumed from a general request.
